Trelent - built for accessibility.

From the user interface to pricing, we want you to be able to use your Trelent PC as easily as possible. Transparent pricing is key to ensuring this, which is why we have created this price sheet for your convenience.


Estimate your costs



Setup fee - $25

We charge a small $25 setup fee to ensure that we can cover internal costs when we first launch your new Trelent PC. Of this fee, $10 goes towards credits immediately available for using your Trelent upon setup.

Storage - 10¢/GB/mo

Like a traditional computer's hard drive (or SSD), a Trelent PC requires it's own internal storage. We charge 10¢ per GB each month. For example, if you would like 128GB of internal storage, it would cost $12.80/month.


Upgrades - $2.50

We charge a $2.50 fee each time you upgrade your Trelent PC. For example, if you change your plan from 8GB RAM and 2 vCPU's to 16GB RAM and 4 vCPU's, you will be charged $2.50 from your credits, and usage will be billed at the new rate.

Usage - starts at 25¢/hr

With Trelent, how much you pay is dictated by how much you use. There are many "plans" each with different resources for different use-cases. Each subsequent plan has double the resources as the one before it, and costs twice as much.
