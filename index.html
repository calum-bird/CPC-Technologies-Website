Questions, anyone?

If you own a computer, you've probably felt it slow down over time. A few too many browser tabs, or one too many apps open, and boom! Your PC is unusable. Trelent let's you run extra programs on a separate, more powerful computer, so that you can multitask without worrying about a slow PC!

Some people use their computer more than others. The average individual will use their computer for several hours each day, and run into slow-downs later in the day. That means they may only need to use their Trelent PC for an hour here or there.

Some power users, on the other hand, may run into slow-downs right from the start, and need near-constant access to their Trelent PC.

Our plans offer cost-efficient solutions for both groups, and everyone in between!
