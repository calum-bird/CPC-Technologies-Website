Cookies

CloudPC uses cookies to serve many purposes. This includes but is not limited to:

  1. Authentication to our services via Auth0
  2. Payment processing via Stripe
  3. Session ID's for CloudPC Dashboard sessions
  4. Realtime notification services via Pusher
  5. Website traffic tracking via Google

Below is a breakdown of each list item, why it is important, and what data specifically is stored in their respective cookie(s).


Authentication

Cookies are essential for authentication. An authentication token and state retrieved from Auth0 is stored in a persistent cookie under the domain cpc.zone, and any of its subdomains. This allows us to offer access to your CloudPC without requiring a second sign-on after clicking "Acess CloudPC" from the CloudPC Dashboard. Additionally, this allows you to access the CloudPC Dashboard without necessarily logging in if you have previously logged in on any given web browser. CPC Technologies does not store your login information on our systems. We delegate authentication to a third party, Auth0, so that we can focus on what we do best, CloudPC, and letting Auth0 do what they do best, authentication. CloudPC follows all Auth0's implementation best practices. Read more about Auth0's use of cookies here.

Auth0 is thoroughly data privacy compliant, and has been awarded certificates from numerous third-party organizations and security audits. Auth0 is GDPR ready, "is certified under the Privacy Shield program", and is "considered a Business Associate as defined by the US HIPAA and HITECH legislation". They also undergo a SOC type II audit annually by an independant third party. To compliment these certifications further, they are also certified under CSA STAR, and have an additional independant annual audit for ISO 27001/27018 certification.

Additional details regarding each of these certifications, and a complete list of compliance are available here. All quotes are from the linked page, dated May 31st, 2020.


Payment processing
